About

During the winter of 2022-3, the cost-of-living crisis in the UK means more-and-more people will struggle to stay warm. Many different groups - from voluntary groups to local councils - have started collating different directories of warm spaces. This duplicates work and means each directory only contains a subset of all warm spaces. So we have developed this as a lower bandwidth tool that searches through multiple warm spaces directories.
